# Callify - Hotel Voice AI Edition

## Core Vision
Callify is exclusively built for Hotels. It acts as a 24/7 AI Voice Receptionist that handles customer calls — informing callers about room availability and hotel details, and (on the Pro plan) processing online payments to reserve rooms.

## Authentication
- Hotel businesses sign up / log in using **Google OAuth** via Supabase Auth.
- The landing page navbar shows **Sign In** (if logged out) or **Dashboard + Sign Out** (if logged in with active plan).
- After authentication, new users are routed to the **Onboarding** flow.
- Returning users with an active plan are sent directly to the **Dashboard**.
- Auth flow: `/auth/login` → Google OAuth popup → `/auth/callback` → redirect to `/onboarding` or `/dashboard`.

## Business ↔ Plan ↔ Number Mapping

### How Mapping Works
- **Primary key for mapping:** The business's **Google email address**.
- When a hotel owner signs in with Google, we look up `businesses.email` to find their record.
- **If no business row exists for that email → the user is NOT mapped.** They have signed in but haven't purchased a plan yet. They get routed to onboarding.
- **A business row is only created during onboarding** (after filling property details + choosing a plan).
- **`plan_status` becomes `active` only after successful Cashfree payment.** Until then, `plan_status = 'pending'` and no number is assigned.

### Number Assignment Logic (Hackathon Demo)
- For the purposes of this demo, we only have **2 fixed Twilio phone numbers** available.
- **Starter plan (Plan 1) → assigned the "Starter Demo" Twilio number:** The AI on this number ONLY handles room availability checks and hotel info/FAQs. It cannot send payment links.
- **Pro plan (Plan 2) → assigned the "Pro Demo" Twilio number:** The AI on this number handles everything — availability, info, AND sends a WhatsApp payment link so the guest can pay to confirm the reservation.
- When a customer calls either number, Twilio routes to our AI which checks the business's plan to determine what capabilities to enable.

### Mapping Table (businesses)
| Column | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `email` (UNIQUE) | Google email — primary mapping key |
| `user_id` (UNIQUE) | Supabase auth user ID |
| `plan` | `'starter'` or `'pro'` — chosen during onboarding |
| `plan_status` | `'pending'` → `'active'` → `'cancelled'` |
| `assigned_number` | Twilio number (NULL until admin assigns) |

### Flow Summary
1. User signs in with Google → email captured from OAuth
2. `/api/auth/me` checks `businesses` table by email
3. No row → user not mapped → redirect to onboarding
4. Row exists + `plan_status = 'active'` → user is mapped → show dashboard
5. Row exists + `plan_status = 'pending'` → payment not complete → resume onboarding

## Pricing Plans (2 Tiers)

### Plan 1 — Starter (₹6/month)
The AI Voice Receptionist handles incoming calls to:
- Tell customers about **room availability** for requested dates.
- Answer **hotel info & FAQs** (pool hours, pet policy, check-in time, parking, etc.) from the hotel's knowledge base.
- Provide a **basic dashboard** for hotel managers to see call activity.
- Email support included.

**What Starter does NOT include:** Online payments, room reservations, or advanced analytics.

### Plan 2 — Pro (₹7/month)
Everything in Starter, plus:
- Customers can **reserve rooms and receive payment links via WhatsApp** directly after the AI call.
- **WhatsApp Payment Links:** The AI triggers an automated WhatsApp message containing a secure payment link (via Cashfree or Stripe) to the caller's mobile number.
- **Full analytics dashboard** with booking conversion rates, peak call hours, and inquiry insights.
- Requires businesses to provide WhatsApp API credentials / Payment Gateway keys.

## The Business Logic (Hotel Focus)

### 1. Room Availability & Hotel Info (Starter + Pro)
- **Real-time Availability:** The AI checks the current inventory of rooms (e.g., standard, deluxe, suite) for specific dates and informs callers.
- **Knowledge Base FAQs:** The AI answers context-aware FAQs based on the hotel's uploaded knowledge base (pet policies, pool timings, breakfast hours, parking availability, etc.).

### 2. Booking & Payment (Pro Plan Only)
- **Reservation Engine:** Customers can book rooms directly through voice. The AI captures Check-in/Check-out dates, guest count, and room preference, executing a 'Pending' booking.
- **Integrated WhatsApp Link:** After booking, the AI automatically sends a WhatsApp message to the caller's number with a secure payment link to collect card details and finalize the booking.
- **Modification & Cancellation:** Customers can call to change dates or cancel bookings.

### 3. Business Dashboard (Web View)
- **Starter:** Basic call log and reservation list view.
- **Pro:** Full analytics — real-time reservations table, room inventory control, call analytics with conversion metrics and inquiry insights.

## System Architecture Updates (MCP Integrations)

The MCP server exposes the following hotel-specific tool interfaces for the LLM to call during a conversation:

- `check_room_availability(check_in_date, check_out_date, room_type, guests)` → Returns available rooms, capacity, and current pricing.
- `search_hotel_knowledge(question)` → Queries the hotel's uploaded PDF/FAQ document for rules and amenities.
- `create_reservation(guest_name, guest_phone, check_in_date, check_out_date, room_type)` → *(Pro only)* Books the room and returns a confirmation/reservation ID.
- `modify_reservation(reservation_id, new_check_in, new_check_out)` → *(Pro only)* Updates the booking in real-time.
- `cancel_reservation(reservation_id)` → *(Pro only)* Cancels the booking and frees up the room inventory.

## Required Onboarding Data (For Hotel Setup)
To deploy the AI Voice Assistant for a specific hotel, the following data is captured during onboarding:
- **Property Details:** hotel_name, hotel_type (e.g., Boutique/Business/Luxury), city, timezone, front_desk_phone.
- **Knowledge Base:** Text or PDF uploads covering hotel rules and amenities.
- **Room Configuration:** Business owners dynamically set their exact room types, pricing, and counts during onboarding. These exact parameters instantly reflect in their dashboard inventory and are fully editable later completely inside the 'Account & AI Settings' view of their dashboard.
- **Integration Keys (Pro only):** WhatsApp API credentials and Payment Gateway keys (Cashfree/Stripe) are required from businesses on Plan 2 to send payment links.

## Example Call Flows

### Starter Plan — Info Call
1. **Customer** dials the hotel's virtual number.
2. **AI Agent:** "Welcome to Sunset Oasis Hotel. How can I help you?"
3. **Customer:** "Do you have a deluxe room available this weekend for two people?"
4. *(AI queries `check_room_availability`)*
5. **AI Agent:** "Yes, we have deluxe rooms available from Friday to Sunday at $150 per night. To make a reservation, please call our front desk at 555-0198 or visit our website."

### Pro Plan — Booking + Payment Call
1. **Customer** dials the hotel's virtual number.
2. **AI Agent:** "Welcome to Sunset Oasis Hotel. How can I assist you today?"
3. **Customer:** "Do you have a deluxe room available this weekend for two people?"
4. *(AI queries `check_room_availability`)*
5. **AI Agent:** "Yes, we have deluxe rooms available from Friday to Sunday. The total rate is $300. Would you like me to book one for you right now?"
6. **Customer:** "Yes, please. My name is John Doe, and my number is 123-456-7890."
7. *(AI executes `create_reservation` with 'Pending' status)*
8. **AI Agent:** "Perfect, John. I have reserved the room for you. I am sending a secure payment link to your WhatsApp number right now. Please complete the payment to confirm your booking."
9. *(System uses hotel's WhatsApp API to send a message containing a Cashfree/Stripe checkout URL)*
10. **Customer:** "Got it, thank you!"

## Technical Restrictions Applied
- The AI's `systemPrompt` is strictly bounded to a hotel persona. It rejects out-of-domain questions.
- If a customer asks a non-hotel question, the AI replies: "I am the virtual concierge for [Hotel Name] and can only assist with room availability, hotel information, and bookings."
- Starter plan AI will not attempt to create reservations or process payments — it directs callers to the front desk or website instead.
- The data models in Supabase reflect `Room`, `Reservation`, `HotelService`, and `CallLog` entities.
